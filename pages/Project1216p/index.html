Man must explore, and this is exploration at its greatest

Problems look mighty small from 150 miles up

Posted by Start Boostrap on August 24, 2014

Never in all their history have men been able truly to conceive of the world as one: a single sphere, a globe, having the qualities of a globe, a round earth in which all the directions eventually meet, in which there is no center because every point or none, is center - an equal earth with all men occupy as equals. The airman's earth, if free men make it, will be truly round: a globe in practice, not in theory.
